Retail Services

Refer Top Products

Tranont pays between 10-30% commission on all Retail Service revenue it receives. The actual percentage is based on the type and number of paid retail service accounts that the Associate has each month. Also, all retail services generate Personal Volume at a rate of 30% of the same revenue received. Ex: If $100 is paid to Tranont each month, the active referring Associate receives 30 points of Personal Volume and the appropriate amount of commission.
