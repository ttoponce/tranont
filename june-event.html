Transportation to Downtown SLC

TRAX Light Rail:

The green line leaves the airport every 15 minutes on weekdays and every 20 minutes on weekends. The stop is located on the south side of Terminal One.

Get off at the Temple Square stop for the Salt Palace.

Price: $2.50

Hours of Operation:

Weekdays: 5:42 a.m. - 11:27 p.m.
Weekends: 6:31 a.m. to 11:11 p.m.

Taxi or other transportation network companies:

Maximum rate of $30 (Not including Uber and Lyft)

The fare should be negotiated before departing the airport.
